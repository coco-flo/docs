## Payment Failure and Dunning

**First failure** — Stripe handles retry automatically. Retry: day 1, 3, 5, 7.

**Repeated failures (3\+)** — Reach out directly. If unresolved after 7 days, suspend in Heimdall.

**Grace period** — 7 days from final failed retry, outreach at day 3.

---

## Disputes and Refunds

**Stripe subscription dispute** — Submit Stripe invoice as evidence immediately. 7-day window.

**Duplicate charge** — Full refund via Stripe dashboard immediately.

**General refunds** — No published policy. Case by case. Document here.
