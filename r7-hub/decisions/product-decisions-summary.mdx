## Core identity (locked)

**Hero headline:** Stop running your business over text. **Tagline:** From quote to paid. Total clarity. **Sub-copy:** Your client signs the scope. You get paid. **ICP:** Trade-neutral. Any solo or small service business owner.

## Primary value proposition (locked)

Most service business owners are stuck between two bad options: simple mobile apps that stop at the invoice (Invoice Simple, Wave, Invoice Fly), and enterprise field service platforms built for dispatch (Jobber $19-$529/mo, HouseCall Pro $79-$329/mo). Clarely fills the gap.

**Pitch order:** (1) Built for your exact stage — not too simple, not too big. (2) One workflow, quote to paid, on your phone. (3) Quotes include a legally binding signature that freezes scope — no competitor offers this at any price.

## Two-workflow structure (locked)

**Flow A — Field service:** Quote → sign → job auto-created → schedule → complete → invoice → payment. **Flow B — Professional services:** Quote → sign → invoice auto-created and sent. No job step.

---

## Settings architecture (locked)

8 sections: Business, Quoting, Invoicing, Notifications, Schedule, Team, Export, Account. Sections below plan tier are locked with upgrade prompt, never hidden.

## Staff portal (locked)
