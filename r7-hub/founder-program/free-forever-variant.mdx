## What it is

Free Forever is a cohort variant for specific individuals — close contacts, advisors, or cases where paying for access doesn't make sense. These founders receive permanent access to the Clarely Free plan with no expectation of conversion to a paid plan.

They are full participants in the founder program: they join the group channel, complete monthly surveys, and provide feedback. They just don't convert to a paid plan at month 6.

## Who gets it

Free Forever is awarded by explicit decision — not the default for any cold outreach. Typical cases:

- Close personal contacts who are genuinely using the product but where charging would be awkward
- Advisors or collaborators contributing meaningfully to the product
- Edge cases where the person's feedback is uniquely valuable but their business doesn't fit the Founder Rate model

Do not offer Free Forever to avoid a difficult conversation about pricing. If someone isn't a good fit for Founder Rate, they should receive standard pricing or a rejection.

## Plan setup

- Stripe: No subscription. No credit card collected.
- App plan: `free`
- `is_early_adopter`: `true`
- `trial_ends_at`: Set to a date 5\+ years in the future
- Heimdall display: "Free" plan with early adopter flag visible

## What they can access

Standard Free tier limits apply: 100 quotes/month cap, 50 client cap, no email delivery, no digital signature. The `is_early_adopter` flag is for internal tracking only — it does not expand feature access.

If a Free Forever founder needs more features, they need a paid plan. Free Forever is not a workaround.

## Acceptance email

Use Variant 2 in [Acceptance email templates](/r7-hub/founder-program/acceptance-email-templates).

## Survey participation

Same 6-month cadence as Founder Rate founders. Compliance is tracked but has no billing consequence.

## Conversion

At month 6, no conversion pitch. Thank them and confirm they remain on Free access. If they express interest in upgrading, direct them to standard plan pricing.
