## When to update the tracker

**At acceptance:** Add row. Status = Active. Start date = account creation date. Leave survey columns empty.

**Each month:** After survey window closes (7-day response window), mark Y or N. Update notes with notable engagement details.

**At month 5:** Mark Month 5 outreach column Y once conversion conversation begins.

**At month 6:** Run compliance audit. Update Compliant column. Set Month 6 outcome.

**On cancellation:** Status = Cancelled. Note date and reason.

## Running the month 6 compliance audit

1. Sort by Start Date. Find all founders where Start Date \+ 180 days is today or earlier.
2. Count Y responses across Month 1-6 survey columns per founder.
3. 5\+ responses: Compliant = Y. No action.
4. 4 or fewer responses: Compliant = N. Initiate personal outreach before any billing action.
5. Allow 7-day grace period.
6. If unresolved: revert to standard pricing in Heimdall.

Outreach message for non-compliant founders:

> "\[Name\], I'm doing the 6-month check-in for the Founder Program. You responded to \[X\] of 6 surveys, and the threshold to keep the Founder Rate is 5. I want to reach out personally before making any change. Is there anything that got in the way? I'd rather hear from you than just flip a switch."

## Handling non-responsive founders

Miss 2 consecutive surveys without group channel response: try direct email. If no response after 2 weeks, mark Status as Non-responsive. Continue sending surveys. Don't remove from the program. Apply normal month 6 audit.

## Triggering month 5 conversion outreach

Begin conversion conversations in parallel with the month 5 survey. Don't wait for the survey response.

> "\[Name\], we're getting close to the end of the founder program. I wanted to touch base before the formal check-in. How has it been going? Are you planning to continue with Clarely after the 6 months?"

Note the outcome in the tracker. If yes, confirm Stripe is configured for billing to start.
