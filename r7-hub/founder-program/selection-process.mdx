## Cohort targets

| Dimension | Target |
| --- | --- |
| Total seats | 25 |
| Max one business type | 35% (9 seats) |
| Solo operators | 10 |
| Small (1–3 team members) | 10 |
| Mid (4–15 team members) | 5 |

**By business type:** Cleaning: 8 · Trades: 8 · Professional services: 5 · Other service: 4

## Batches

| Batch | Size | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 5 | Warm contacts, all solo |
| 2 | 8 | Cold expansion begins |
| 3 | 7 | — |

Batch timing is a judgment call based on app stability. Do not rush to Batch 2 until Batch 1 is stable and actively using the product.

## Application form

Built in Tally.so (OPS-8). Share via DM only — not posted publicly.

Application fields: name, business name, business type, team size, email, and three questions: (1) How do you currently manage quotes and invoices? (2) What percentage of your quote-to-invoice volume are you willing to run through Clarely during the program? (3) What made you interested in Clarely?

## Evaluation criteria

1. Willing to run 50%\+ of quote-to-invoice volume through Clarely during founder period
2. Business type and size fit cohort composition targets
3. Has existing clients and active quoting/invoicing workflow
4. Response quality — thoughtful answers signal genuine engagement
5. Check current cohort tracker before accepting — don't exceed composition targets

## Rejection language

> "Thank you for your interest in Clarely's Founder Program. At this time you don't fit the current profile we're building — it's not a reflection on your business, just the specific mix we're looking for right now. We'll keep your information and reach out if an opportunity opens up."

## Acceptance workflow

1. Review submission against [cohort tracker guide](/r7-hub/founder-program/cohort-tracker-guide) — confirm slot available
2. Confirm acceptance decision
3. Determine cohort type: Founder Rate or Free Forever (see [Free Forever variant](/r7-hub/founder-program/free-forever-variant))
4. Send appropriate [acceptance email](/r7-hub/founder-program/acceptance-email-templates)
5. Await explicit agreement (email reply or group channel "I'm in")
6. Create account or provide signup link
7. In Heimdall: set plan to `business` (Founder Rate) or `free` (Free Forever), set `is_early_adopter = true` **Note:** CLA-6 and CLA-8 must be shipped before this step is operational.
8. Set `trial_ends_at` to a date 5\+ years in the future
9. Update cohort tracker: Status = Active, start date, plan type, batch, CLR account number
10. Send [onboarding checklist](/r7-hub/founder-program/founder-onboarding-checklist)
11. Add to founder group channel
12. Set calendar reminder for Month 1 survey (start date \+ 30 days)

## Volume commitment

Founders commit to running 50% of their quote-to-invoice volume through Clarely. Soft commitment — tracked via monthly surveys, not enforced programmatically.

## Communicating exclusivity

When reaching out cold: "Founder access is limited to 25 accounts. We're building this with a small group of real service business owners before opening it up." Do not manufacture urgency. The exclusivity is real.
