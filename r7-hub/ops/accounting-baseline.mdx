## Tax considerations

**Entity type:** Clarely LLC files as a single-member LLC (Schedule C). Profit flows through to personal return.

**Two active Florida entities:** Clarely LLC and Scrub Hub Crew both file as Schedule C. Consult accountant before first filing. Do not assume you can combine them without guidance.

**Self-employment tax:** 15.3% on net profit (12.4% Social Security \+ 2.9% Medicare). At $1K MRR ($12K ARR), roughly \$1,836 in SE tax at 100% profit margin.

**Home office deduction:** If you have a dedicated workspace, document square footage before tax season.

**Stripe 1099-K:** Stripe issues a 1099-K for accounts exceeding \$600 in annual payments. Confirm you receive it and that amounts match your records.
