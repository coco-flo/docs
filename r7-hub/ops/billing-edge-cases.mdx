Records decisions made about billing edge cases for consistent future handling.

**Scope note:** "Billing" here refers exclusively to Clarely subscription billing (business owners paying for their Clarely plan via Stripe). Client payments via Venmo, Cash App, Zelle are outside Clarely's billing system.

---

## Founder Rate edge cases

**Returning after cancellation:** Founder Rate does not automatically reinstate. Returning founders receive standard pricing. Exceptions require explicit founder approval. Document any exceptions here.

**Upgrade request:** Founders cannot upgrade beyond Founder Rate (fixed at Business-tier). Log the request and route to Prod.

**Survey non-compliance at month 6:** Fewer than 5 of 6 surveys → personal outreach before any billing action. 7-day grace period after month 6 survey window. If unresolved, revert to standard pricing in Heimdall.

---

## Payment failure scenarios

**First failure:** Stripe handles retry automatically. Don't intervene unless founder contacts support. Default retry: day 1, 3, 5, 7.

**Repeated failures (3\+):** Reach out via group channel. Offer to update payment method. If unresolved after 7 days: suspend in Heimdall.

Suspension process:

1. Open Heimdall and locate the account
2. Suspend with reason: `payment_failure` and notes referencing outreach attempts
3. Send email: "Your Clarely account has been temporarily suspended due to payment failure. Please update your payment method at clarely.app/settings/account to restore access. Reply if you need help."
4. Action is logged in Heimdall audit trail automatically

**Data on suspension:** Fully preserved. Owner cannot log in but no data is deleted. Reactivate via Heimdall when resolved.

---

## Dispute handling

**Stripe subscription dispute:** Provide the Stripe invoice as primary evidence immediately. Dispute window is typically 7 days — do not wait. These are Clarely subscription billing disputes, not client disputes about work performed.

**Duplicate charge:** Issue full refund via Stripe dashboard immediately.

---

## Refunds

No published policy. Handle case by case. Document all decisions here.

---

## Plan changes and data

**Upgrade:** Takes effect immediately.

**Downgrade:** Takes effect at end of current billing cycle.

**Cancellation:** Account moves to Free plan at end of billing period. All data retained indefinitely.

**Data on cancellation:** Retained unless the owner requests deletion. No automatic deletion. Accounts suspended for 12 months become eligible for manual purge via Heimdall.

---

## Free Forever edge cases

**Access questions:** Free Forever founders are on the standard free plan with Free-tier feature limits. `is_early_adopter` is for tracking only — it does not expand feature access. If a Free Forever founder needs more features, they need a paid plan.

**Compliance failure:** No billing consequence. Track for product feedback purposes only.
