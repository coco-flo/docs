Jobs are created from accepted quotes. Once a client signs, you can convert the quote to a job and schedule it in minutes.

## Create a job from a quote

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the accepted quote">
    Go to **Quotes**, find the accepted quote, and tap **Create Job**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the date and time">
    Pick the date and start time for the job. Add an end time or duration if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the location">
    Enter the job address. This is the on-site location where the work happens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign team members (optional)">
    Add one or more team members to the job. See [Assign team members](/jobs/assign-team-member).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Tap **Save**. The job is created and appears on your schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a scheduled job

You can edit the date, time, location, and team assignment on any job that hasn't been completed. Open the job and tap **Edit**.

## Job details

Every job carries the full context from its originating quote — client name, line items, and total. You don't need to re-enter any of that information.
