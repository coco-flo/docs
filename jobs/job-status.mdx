---

Every job in Clarely has a status that reflects where it is in the workflow. Keeping statuses current gives you and your team an accurate picture of what's happening.

## Status definitions

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Draft | Job created but not yet scheduled |
| Scheduled | Date and time set, work hasn't started |
| In Progress | Work is actively underway |
| Completed | Work is done — ready to invoice |
| Cancelled | Job will not be completed |

## Updating a status

Open any job and tap the status badge to change it. Clarely steps you through the sequence — you can't skip from Scheduled to Completed without going through In Progress, but you can move backward if needed.

## Completing a job

When you mark a job **Completed**, Clarely prompts you to generate an invoice. You can generate immediately or come back to it later from the job detail page.

<Note>
  Completing a job doesn't automatically send an invoice. It makes the invoice available to generate. You still control when the invoice goes to the client.
</Note>

## Cancelled jobs

Cancelling a job removes it from your active schedule. The originating quote remains in your system for reference. Cancelled jobs can be reopened if needed.
