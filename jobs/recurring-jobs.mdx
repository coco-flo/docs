Recurring jobs let you set up a repeating schedule for clients who need regular service — weekly cleaning, monthly maintenance, quarterly inspections, and so on.

## Setting up a recurring job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open an accepted quote">
    Go to the accepted quote for the client you want to set up on a recurring schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create job and enable recurrence">
    Tap **Create Job**, then toggle on **Recurring**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the schedule">
    Choose the frequency: weekly, biweekly, monthly, or custom interval. Set the start date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set an end date (optional)">
    Add an end date if the recurring schedule has a defined end. Leave it open-ended for ongoing service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Tap **Save**. Clarely creates the first job and schedules the rest automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How recurring jobs work

Each occurrence is a separate job in your schedule. When one job is completed, the next one is already on your calendar. Each completed job can generate its own invoice.

## Editing a recurring schedule

You can edit a single occurrence without affecting the rest of the series, or edit the entire series from any occurrence forward. Open the job and tap **Edit** — Clarely asks which jobs to apply the change to.

## Pausing or cancelling a recurring schedule

Open any job in the series and tap **Cancel Series** to stop all future occurrences. To pause temporarily, cancel future occurrences individually and add them back manually when ready to resume.
