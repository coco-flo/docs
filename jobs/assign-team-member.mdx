You can assign team members to any job so everyone knows where they need to be and when.

## Assigning from a job

Open a job and tap **Assign Team Member**. Select one or more team members from your team list. They receive a notification with the job details.

## Who can be assigned

Any active team member on your Clarely account can be assigned to a job. Team members must be added to your account first — see [Team members](/account/team-members).

## What team members see

Assigned team members see the job on their schedule with the date, time, location, and client name. They can update the job status from their own device.

## Removing an assignment

Open the job, find the assigned team member, and tap the remove icon. The team member is notified that they've been removed from the job.

## Assigning multiple team members

You can assign as many team members as needed to a single job. All assigned members see the job on their schedule.
