The team view gives you a single view of all scheduled jobs across every team member, so you can see who's working where and when without digging through individual schedules.

## Accessing team view

From the Jobs section, tap the **Team** tab at the top of the screen. Jobs are displayed in a calendar view grouped by team member.

## Filtering the view

You can filter by date range, team member, or job status. Use the filters to focus on what's relevant — today's jobs, a specific team member's week, or all jobs with a particular status.

## Schedule view vs list view

Toggle between calendar and list view depending on what you need. Calendar view shows jobs spatially across days. List view shows jobs in order with more detail per row.

## Spotting conflicts

The team view makes scheduling conflicts visible at a glance. If two jobs for the same team member overlap, they appear stacked in the calendar view. Resolve conflicts by reassigning one job to another team member or adjusting the schedule.
