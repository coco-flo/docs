If your client says they didn't receive the quote email, work through these steps in order.

## 1. Ask them to check spam

Quote emails come from Clarely's sending domain. Many email clients — especially business email hosted on Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 — apply aggressive spam filtering to emails from new senders. Ask your client to check their spam or junk folder and look for an email from Clarely.

If they find it in spam, have them mark it as Not Spam so future emails land in their inbox.

## 2. Verify the email address

Open the quote in Clarely and check the client's email address. A single typo means the email went somewhere else entirely. If the address is wrong:

1. Go to **Clients** and update the client's email
2. Open the quote and tap **Resend**

## 3. Resend the quote

Open the quote and tap **Resend**. A new email goes to the address on file. This sends a fresh copy — the signing link is the same.

## 4. Share the link directly

If email is proving unreliable for this client, you can share the quote link directly via text or messaging app. Open the sent quote and look for a **Copy Link** or **Share** option. The link goes directly to the quote signing page — the client can open it in any browser.

## 5. Have them add Clarely to their contacts

For clients you quote regularly, suggest they add Clarely's sending address to their contacts. This prevents future emails from going to spam.

## Still not receiving it?

Email [support@clarely.co](mailto:support@clarely.co) with the client's email address and the quote number. We can check whether the send was attempted and whether it bounced.
