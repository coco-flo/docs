## Client says they paid but invoice still shows Sent

Stripe processes payments in real time. If the invoice still shows Sent after your client says they paid:

1. Check your Stripe dashboard for the transaction
2. If the payment shows in Stripe, it may take a few minutes to sync to Clarely — refresh the invoice
3. If the payment doesn't show in Stripe, the transaction may not have completed — ask the client to try again

## Client's payment is failing

Ask the client to:

- Double-check their card number, expiry, and CVV
- Make sure their billing address matches what's on file with their bank
- Try a different card
- Contact their bank — some banks block online transactions by default

If they continue to have trouble, they can contact their bank to authorize the transaction, then retry.

## Payment processed but funds haven't arrived

Stripe's standard payout is 2 business days after payment. If it's been longer:

1. Log in to your Stripe dashboard and check payout status
2. Verify your bank account details in Stripe are correct
3. Check if Stripe has flagged your account for review — new accounts sometimes have a longer initial hold

## Invoice was paid twice

Contact [support@clarely.co](mailto:support@clarely.co) immediately with the invoice number. We'll help coordinate with Stripe for a refund of the duplicate charge.

## I need to refund a client

Refunds are issued through Stripe. Log in to your Stripe dashboard, find the payment, and issue a full or partial refund from there. The invoice status in Clarely doesn't update automatically for refunds — mark it manually if needed.
