**Can I create a job without a quote?**

No. Jobs in Clarely are created from accepted quotes. This ensures every job has a signed scope before work begins.

**Can I reschedule a job?**

Yes. Open the job and tap **Edit** to change the date, time, or duration. Rescheduling doesn't affect the originating quote or the signed scope.

**What happens if I need to cancel a job?**

Open the job and tap **Cancel**. Cancelled jobs are removed from the active schedule. The originating quote stays in your system. You can reopen a cancelled job if needed.

**Can two team members be assigned to the same job?**

Yes. Assign as many team members as needed. All assigned members see the job on their schedule.

**Can a team member see jobs they're not assigned to?**

No. Team members only see jobs assigned to them. You see all jobs across your entire team.

**How do recurring jobs handle scheduling conflicts?**

Clarely doesn't automatically check for conflicts on recurring jobs. Review your team view after setting up a recurring schedule to make sure there are no overlaps.

**Can I add notes to a job?**

Yes. Open the job and add internal notes in the Notes field. Notes are visible to you and your team but not to clients.

**What happens to a job if the client cancels?**

Cancel the job manually. If the work hasn't been invoiced, no payment is processed. If an invoice was already sent, you'll need to void or write it off separately.
