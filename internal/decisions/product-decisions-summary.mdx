## Core identity (locked)

**Hero headline:** Stop running your business over text.

**Tagline:** From quote to paid. Total clarity.

**Sub-copy:** Your client signs the scope. You get paid.

**ICP:** Trade-neutral. Any solo or small service business owner. No vertical excluded at launch.

## Primary value proposition (locked)

Most service business owners are stuck between two bad options: simple mobile apps that stop at the invoice (Invoice Simple, Wave, Invoice Fly), and enterprise field service platforms built for dispatch and crew management (Jobber $19-$529/mo, HouseCall Pro $79-$329/mo). Nothing affordable and complete exists in the middle.

Clarely fills that gap. Everything a small service business needs that the simple apps can't do, without the overhead of platforms built for businesses ten times the size.

**The pitch order:**

1. Built for your exact stage — not too simple, not too big
2. One workflow, quote to paid, on your phone, priced for a one-person operation
3. Quotes include a legally binding signature that freezes scope — no competitor offers this at any price

**Durable differentiator:** Legally binding digital signatures with frozen scope protection. When a client accepts a quote, the exact terms are frozen permanently. No competitor offers this at any price point. This answers "how is your quote different?" — it is not the opening argument.

## Two-workflow structure (locked)

**Flow A — Field service (default):** Quote → client signs → job auto-created → schedule → complete → invoice → payment. Use for: cleaning, trades, maintenance, any on-site work.

**Flow B — Professional services:** Quote → client signs → invoice auto-created and sent immediately. No job step. Use for: consulting, design, remote work.

Toggle is set per quote. Default configurable per account. Flow A is the system default.

## Settings architecture (locked)

8 sections. Mobile: home screen list. Desktop: persistent left sidebar. Sections below plan tier are locked with upgrade prompt, never hidden.

| Section | Key contents |
| --- | --- |
| Business | Name, slug, logo, address, phone, payment handles, payment notes |
| Quoting | Tax rate, contract terms, acceptance flow default, quote expiry, deposit default, follow-up |
| Invoicing | Net terms, auto-invoice toggle, invoice reminders, late fee, footer text |
| Notifications | Email and SMS toggles (SMS is Business\+) |
| Schedule | Default job duration, calendar hours, iCal token (Team\+) |
| Team | Team member list, invite, disable |
| Export | 8 export types (Team\+ plan) |
| Account | Profile, password, plan/billing, referral (Starter\+), account deletion |

## Staff portal (locked)

Team members log in via `clarely.app/[business-slug]`. Email \+ 6-digit OTP per session. Sessions last 10 hours. No password. Team members see only assigned jobs.
