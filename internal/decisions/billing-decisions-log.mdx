## Active decisions

**Founder Rate pricing** — decided May 2026
$20/month, all features, "for as long as Clarely operates this plan." Internal Stripe product, not visible in customer portal. Maps to Business-tier feature access. Displays as "Founder" in all in-product UI.

**Founder Rate free period** — decided May 2026
6-month free period before billing starts. No credit card required during free period. Billing begins automatically at month 6 via Stripe subscription.

**Survey compliance threshold** — decided May 2026
80% (5 of 6 monthly surveys) required to retain Founder Rate at 6-month mark. Non-compliant founders revert to standard pricing after a 7-day grace period and personal outreach.

**Founder Rate on return** — decided May 2026
Founders who cancel and return are offered standard pricing. Founder Rate does not automatically reinstate. Exceptions approved case by case.

**Dispute response** — decided May 2026
Provide the signed quote as primary evidence in all Stripe disputes. Submit immediately within Stripe's dispute window. No waiting period.

**Duplicate payment** — decided May 2026
Issue full refund of duplicate charge immediately via Stripe dashboard. No waiting period.

**Grace period for failed payments** — decided May 2026
Stripe handles automatic retry. Manual intervention only if founder contacts support. If 3+ failures with no contact, reach out directly. Suspend access after 7 days of no resolution.
