## Founder Rate

**Pricing** — May 2026. \$20/month, Business-tier feature access, "for as long as Clarely operates this plan." Internal Stripe product, not visible in customer portal. Displays as "Founder" in all in-product UI.

**Free period** — May 2026. 6 months free before billing starts. No credit card required during free period. Billing begins automatically at month 6 via Stripe subscription.

**Survey compliance** — May 2026. 80% (5 of 6 monthly surveys) required to retain Founder Rate at month 6. Non-compliant founders revert to standard pricing after 7-day grace period and personal outreach.

**On return after cancellation** — May 2026. Founder Rate does not automatically reinstate. Returning founders are offered standard pricing. Case-by-case exceptions at founder discretion.

**Upgrade requests** — May 2026. Founders cannot upgrade beyond Founder Rate. Fixed at Business-tier access. Log requests and route to Prod for roadmap consideration.

## Free Forever Cohort

**Definition** — May 2026. A second cohort variant for close contacts or specific cases where no payment is expected. Accounts placed directly on the `free` plan with `is_early_adopter = true`. No Stripe subscription created. See [Free Forever variant](/r7-hub/founder-program/free-forever-variant) for full details.

**Survey participation** — May 2026. Free Forever founders participate in the 6-month survey cadence on the same schedule. Non-compliance does not affect billing (no billing exists) but is tracked for product feedback purposes.

## Payment Failure and Dunning

**First failure** — May 2026. Stripe handles retry automatically. Do not intervene unless founder contacts support. Stripe default retry schedule: day 1, 3, 5, 7 after initial failure.

**Repeated failures (3\+)** — May 2026. Reach out directly via the founder group channel. Offer to update payment method. If unresolved after 7 days, suspend account access via Heimdall. Communicate suspension reason clearly.

**Grace period** — May 2026. 7 days from final failed retry attempt, with direct outreach at day 3.

## Disputes and Refunds

**Stripe subscription dispute** — May 2026. Applies to Clarely plan billing disputes only. Submit Stripe invoice as primary evidence immediately — Stripe dispute window is typically 7 days. Do not wait.

**Duplicate charge** — May 2026. Issue full refund via Stripe dashboard immediately. No waiting period.

**General refunds** — May 2026. No published policy. Handle case by case. Document all decisions here.

## Plan Changes

**Upgrades** — decided. Take effect immediately.

**Downgrades** — decided. Take effect at end of current billing cycle. Data retained. Features above new tier become unavailable at next billing date.

**Cancellation** — decided. Account moves to Free plan at end of billing period. All data retained. No automatic deletion.

**Data on cancellation** — decided. Customer data retained indefinitely after cancellation unless owner requests deletion. Suspension for non-payment does not delete data — it blocks login until resolved, or until the 12-month suspension-to-purge timeline is reached.
