This document records decisions for billing edge cases. Each entry is logged so future handling is consistent.

**Scope note:** "Billing" here refers exclusively to Clarely subscription billing (business owners paying for their Clarely plan via Stripe). Client payments via Venmo, Cash App, Zelle are outside Clarely's billing system entirely.

---

## Founder Rate edge cases

**Founder returning after cancellation:** Founder Rate does not automatically reinstate. Returning founders are offered standard pricing. Exceptions require explicit approval. Document any exceptions here.

**Founder Rate upgrade request:** Fixed at Business-tier access. Log the request and route to Prod for roadmap consideration.

**Survey non-compliance at month 6:** Personal outreach before any billing action. Grace period: 7 days after month 6 survey window closes. If unresolved, revert to standard pricing in Heimdall.

---

## Payment failure scenarios

**First payment failure:** Stripe handles retry automatically. Do not intervene unless the founder contacts support. Stripe default retry: day 1, 3, 5, 7.

**Repeated failures (3\+):** Reach out via group channel. Offer to update payment method. If unresolved after 7 days: suspend access in Heimdall.

Suspension process:

1. Open Heimdall and locate the account
2. Suspend with reason: `payment_failure` and notes referencing outreach attempts
3. Email: "Your Clarely account has been temporarily suspended due to payment failure. Please update your payment method at clarely.app/settings/account to restore access."
4. Log action in Heimdall audit trail

**Account data on suspension:** Fully preserved. Owner cannot log in but no data is deleted. Reactivate via Heimdall once payment is resolved.

---

## Dispute handling

**Stripe dispute filed:** Provide the Stripe invoice as primary evidence immediately. Dispute window is typically 7 days — do not wait.

**Duplicate charge:** Issue full refund via Stripe dashboard immediately.

---

## Refunds

No published refund policy. Handle case by case. Document all decisions here.

---

## Plan changes and data

**Plan upgrade:** Takes effect immediately.

**Plan downgrade:** Takes effect at end of current billing cycle.

**Cancellation:** Account moves to Free plan at end of billing period. All data retained indefinitely.

**Data on cancellation:** Retained unless owner explicitly requests deletion. Accounts suspended for 12 months become eligible for purge via Heimdall — purge is manual and admin-initiated.

---

## Free Forever edge cases

**Access questions:** Free Forever founders are on standard Free tier limits. Free Forever is not a workaround for paid features.

**Non-compliance:** No billing consequence. Track in cohort tracker for product feedback purposes only.
