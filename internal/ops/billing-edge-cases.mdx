This document records decisions made about billing edge cases. Each decision is logged with its rationale so future handling is consistent.

## Founder Rate decisions

**Founder returning after cancellation**
Decision: Founder Rate does not automatically reinstate on return. Founders who cancel and return are offered standard pricing unless a specific exception is approved by the founder.

**Founder Rate on plan upgrade attempt**
Decision: Founders cannot upgrade within the Founder Rate. The Founder Rate is fixed at Business-tier access. If a founder wants features not included, note the request and route to Prod.

**Survey non-compliance**
Decision: Founders must complete 5 of 6 monthly surveys (80% threshold) to retain Founder Rate at the 6-month mark. Non-compliant founders receive personal outreach before automatic reversion. Grace period: 7 days after month 6 survey window closes.

## Payment failure decisions

**First payment failure**
Decision: Stripe handles retry automatically. Do not manually intervene unless the founder contacts support.

**Repeated payment failures (3+)**
Decision: Reach out to founder directly via the founder group channel. Offer to update payment method. If unresolved after 7 days, suspend account access pending resolution.

## Dispute handling

**Stripe dispute filed**
Decision: Provide the signed quote as primary evidence in all disputes. The signed quote with timestamp and IP record is the primary defense. Submit immediately — Stripe dispute window is tight.

**Duplicate payment**
Decision: Issue full refund of the duplicate charge via Stripe dashboard immediately. No waiting period. Update the invoice status manually in Clarely after refund is confirmed.

## Refund policy

No formal refund policy published. Handle case by case. Document all refund decisions here.
