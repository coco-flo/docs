## Platform decision

Pending: Wave (free) or QuickBooks Self-Employed (~$15/month). Decision tracked in OPS-5.

## Minimum setup required

Once bank account is open (OPS-3):

1. Connect Clarely LLC bank account
2. Connect Stripe as revenue source
3. Create expense categories:
   - Infrastructure (Lovable, Resend, forwardemail)
   - Domain / DNS (Route 53, domain registrations)
   - Software Subscriptions (Linear, Better Stack, Mintlify, Tally)
   - Professional Services (accountant, legal if applicable)
4. Confirm Stripe payouts are captured as revenue correctly

## Fixed monthly costs reference

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Lovable | $88/month |
| forwardemail.net | ~$3/month |
| Route 53 / domains | ~$5/month |
| **Total fixed** | ~$96/month |

Break-even: One paying Starter customer ($15/month) does not cover fixed costs. Two Starter customers cover fixed costs with margin.

## Tax considerations

Clarely LLC files as a single-member LLC (Schedule C). Consult accountant on:
- Interaction with Scrub Hub Crew Schedule C on same return
- Quarterly estimated tax payments
- Home office deduction eligibility
- Stripe 1099-K reporting thresholds
