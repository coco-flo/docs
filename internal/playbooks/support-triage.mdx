## Triage decision tree

**App broken or not working as expected?**

- Yes: Create CLA- issue in Linear. Priority: Urgent if blocking a core workflow (can't send quote, can't sign, can't invoice). High otherwise. Acknowledge the founder within 2 hours.

**How-to question?**

- Yes: Answer directly. Link to docs.clarely.co if covered. If not covered, answer and create a doc update task.

**Billing or account issue?**

- Yes: Handle directly. Check Stripe for subscription/payment status. Check Heimdall for account status and plan. Resolve at founder level.

**Feature request?**

- Yes: Log it. Create CLA- issue with Founder Feedback label. Route to Prod for roadmap consideration. Do not commit to timelines.

**Third-party service issue (Venmo, Cash App, Zelle, their email provider)?**

- Yes: Acknowledge. Clarify what you can and can't control. Suggest workarounds. You cannot fix third-party services.

## Template responses

**Bug acknowledged, investigating:**

> "On it. I can reproduce this on my end. I'll have an update for you within \[timeframe\]."

**Bug fixed:**

> "This is fixed. Let me know if you see it again."

**Bug logged, not yet prioritized:**

> "Logged with high priority. I'll follow up once it's fixed. Thanks for the detail."

**Feature request acknowledged:**

> "Good idea. Logged. Not in the immediate roadmap but I want to capture it. I'll follow up if and when it's scoped."

**How-to question with docs link:**

> "\[Answer\]. More detail here: \[link to specific doc page\]."

**Third-party issue:**

> "That's a \[Venmo/Cash App/Zelle\] issue outside of Clarely's control. As a workaround, \[suggestion\]. Consider setting up a second payment handle as backup."

**Account/billing question:**

> "Looking into this now. I'll check the account on our end and follow up shortly."

## Linear issue creation from support

Every bug from a founder becomes a CLA- issue. Include:

- Founder name (in description, not title)
- Steps to reproduce
- Expected vs actual behavior
- Device and OS
- Founder Feedback label
- Link to Tally form submission if available
