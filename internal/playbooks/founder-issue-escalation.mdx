## Escalation triggers

**Data loss or corruption** — Founder reports missing quotes, jobs, invoices, or signed scope data. Stop normal operations. Open Heimdall and check the account directly. If data is genuinely missing, escalate to AppDev immediately (Urgent priority). Communicate to the founder within 1 hour.

**Signed quote data missing or corrupted** — Most critical issue type. A signed quote's frozen scope is a legal record. Escalate to AppDev immediately with Urgent priority. Takes priority over everything else.

**Account inaccessible** — Founder cannot log in and it's not a simple login issue. Check Supabase Auth for account status. Check Heimdall for suspension status. Reset or reactivate within 2 hours.

**Clarely subscription payment issue** — Founder's plan payment failed or was charged incorrectly. Check Stripe. Resolve at founder level. See [Billing edge cases](/r7-hub/ops/billing-edge-cases). Note: this is about Clarely subscription billing, not client payments via Venmo/Cash App/Zelle.

**App completely down** — clarely.app inaccessible for multiple users. Check UptimeRobot alert. Check Supabase status page. Post immediately in the founder group channel. Escalate to AppDev.

## Communication during escalation

Communicate at every step. Silence is worse than bad news.

**Initial acknowledgment (within 1 hour):**

> "I see the issue. This is on my radar and I'm working on it now. I'll have an update for you within \[timeframe\]."

**Progress update (if no resolution by initial timeframe):**

> "Still working on \[issue\]. Current status: \[brief status\]. Next update by \[time\]. I haven't forgotten about you."

**Resolution:**

> "This is resolved. Here's what happened: \[brief explanation\]. Here's what I did to fix it: \[action taken\]. Let me know if you see anything else."

**Partial fix:**

> "I have a partial fix in place. \[What's working now\]. The remaining part is \[what's outstanding\] and I expect to have it resolved by \[date\]. I'll follow up."

---

## Post-resolution process

1. Update the Linear issue with full resolution notes and root cause
2. Update the known issues log in the [support triage playbook](/r7-hub/playbooks/support-triage)
3. Identify if a doc page needs to be created or updated
4. Assess whether a product fix is needed to prevent recurrence. If yes, create a follow-up CLA- issue.
5. Consider whether the affected founder deserves a goodwill gesture. Data loss or extended downtime warrants compensation. Not every bug does.

---

## Accessing Heimdall for account investigation

1. Open Heimdall at heimdall.clargate.tools
2. Log in with super\_admin credentials (Bitwarden)
3. Search for the account by email or CLR account number
4. Check: plan, trial status, suspension status, account dates
5. For data questions: check Supabase directly if Heimdall doesn't show what you need

Every action in Heimdall is audit-logged automatically.
