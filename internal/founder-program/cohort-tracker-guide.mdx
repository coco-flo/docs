## When to update the tracker

**At acceptance:** Add row with all known fields. Status = Active. Start date = account creation date. Leave survey columns empty.

**Each month:** After each survey window closes (allow 7-day response window), mark Y or N for that month. Update notes with anything notable from that founder's engagement.

**At month 5:** Mark Month 5 outreach column Y once conversion conversation begins. Note outcome in Notes.

**At month 6:** Run compliance audit. Update Compliant column. Set Month 6 outcome. If reverting to standard pricing, note date and outreach sent.

**On churn or cancellation:** Update Status to Cancelled. Note date and reason in Notes.

## Running the month 6 compliance audit

1. Sort tracker by Start Date. Find all founders where Start Date \+ 180 days is today or earlier.
2. For each: count Y responses across Month 1-6 survey columns.
3. Founders with 5\+ responses: Compliant = Y. No action required.
4. Founders with 4 or fewer responses: Compliant = N. Initiate personal outreach before taking any billing action.
5. Allow 7-day grace period after outreach.
6. If no resolution: revert to standard pricing in Heimdall.

Personal outreach message for non-compliant founders:

> "\[Name\], I'm doing the 6-month check-in for the Founder Program. You responded to \[X\] of 6 surveys, and the threshold to keep the Founder Rate is 5. I want to reach out personally before making any change. Is there anything that got in the way? I'd rather hear from you than just flip a switch."

## Handling non-responsive founders

If a founder misses 2 consecutive surveys and can't be reached in the group channel:

1. Try email directly to their account email.
2. If still no response after 2 weeks, mark Status as Non-responsive in the tracker.
3. Continue sending surveys. Don't remove them from the program.
4. At month 6, apply normal compliance audit.

Don't escalate a non-responsive founder to cancelled unless they explicitly ask to leave or miss all 6 surveys.

## Reviewing cohort composition

The summary section at the top of the tracker shows current counts by type and size against targets. Check before accepting new founders to avoid exceeding composition targets.

## Triggering month 5 conversion outreach

Month 5 is when personal conversion conversations begin in parallel with the survey. Don't wait for the survey response. Reach out directly:

> "\[Name\], we're getting close to the end of the founder program. I wanted to touch base before the formal check-in. How has it been going? Are you planning to continue with Clarely after the 6 months?"

The goal is to have a committed yes or no before month 6 closes. Note the conversation outcome in the tracker.
