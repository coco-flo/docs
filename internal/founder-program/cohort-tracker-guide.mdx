## When to update

**At acceptance:** Add row, Status = Active, leave survey columns empty.

**Each month:** After the 7-day survey window closes, mark Y or N. Update notes if anything notable happened.

**At month 5:** Mark outreach column Y once the conversion conversation begins.

**At month 6:** Run compliance audit. Update Compliant column and Month 6 outcome.

**On cancellation:** Update Status to Cancelled with date and reason.

## Month 6 compliance audit

1. Sort by Start Date. Find all founders where Start Date \+ 180 days is today or earlier.
2. Count Y responses for each founder across survey columns.
3. 5\+ responses: Compliant = Y. No action.
4. 4 or fewer: Compliant = N. Send personal outreach before any billing action.
5. Allow 7-day grace period after outreach.
6. If unresolved: revert to standard pricing in Heimdall.

Outreach message for non-compliant founders:

> "\[Name\], I'm doing the 6-month check-in for the Founder Program. You responded to \[X\] of 6 surveys, and the threshold to keep the Founder Rate is 5. I want to reach out personally before making any change. Is there anything that got in the way? I'd rather hear from you than just flip a switch."

## Non-responsive founders

Miss 2 consecutive surveys and unreachable in group channel:

1. Email directly to account email.
2. If no response after 2 weeks: mark Status = Non-responsive.
3. Continue sending surveys. Apply normal month 6 audit.

Don't escalate to Cancelled unless they explicitly ask to leave or miss all 6 surveys.

## Cohort composition check

The summary section at the top of the tracker shows current counts against targets. Check before accepting any new founder.

## Month 5 conversion outreach

Begin in parallel with the month 5 survey — don't wait for the survey response.

> "\[Name\], we're getting close to the end of the founder program. I wanted to touch base before the formal check-in. How has it been going? Are you planning to continue with Clarely after the 6 months?"

Goal: have a committed yes or no before month 6 closes. Log outcome in Notes.
