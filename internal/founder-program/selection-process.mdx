## Application form

Built in Tally.so (OPS-8). Share via DM only — not posted publicly.

Fields: name, business name, business type, team size, email. Questions: "How do you currently manage quotes and invoices?", "What percentage of your quote-to-invoice volume are you willing to run through Clarely during the program?", "What made you interested in Clarely?"

## Evaluation criteria

1. Willing to run 50%\+ of quote-to-invoice volume through Clarely during the program
2. Business type and size fit current cohort composition targets
3. Has existing clients and an active quoting/invoicing workflow
4. Response quality — thoughtful answers signal genuine engagement
5. Check cohort tracker before accepting — don't exceed composition targets

## Rejection language

> "Thank you for your interest in Clarely's Founder Program. At this time you don't fit the current profile we're building — it's not a reflection on your business, just the specific mix we're looking for right now. We'll keep your information and reach out if an opportunity opens up."

## Acceptance workflow

1. Review submission against [cohort tracker](/r7-hub/founder-program/cohort-tracker-guide) — confirm slot available
2. Confirm acceptance decision
3. Determine cohort type: Founder Rate or Free Forever (see [Free Forever variant](/r7-hub/founder-program/free-forever-variant))
4. Send appropriate [acceptance email](/r7-hub/founder-program/acceptance-email-templates)
5. Await agreement confirmation (email reply or explicit "I'm in" in group channel)
6. Create account — provide signup link or create manually
7. In Heimdall: set plan to `business` (Founder Rate) or `free` (Free Forever), set `is_early_adopter = true` **Note:** CLA-6 and CLA-8 must be shipped before this step is operational.
8. Set `trial_ends_at` to a date 5\+ years in the future
9. Update cohort tracker: Status = Active, start date, plan type, batch, CLR account number
10. Send [welcome message](/r7-hub/founder-program/founder-onboarding-checklist)
11. Add to founder group channel
12. Set calendar reminders: Month 1 survey (day 30), Month 5 outreach (day 150), Month 6 audit (day 180)

## Volume commitment

Founders commit to running 50% of their quote-to-invoice volume through Clarely. Soft commitment — tracked via monthly surveys, not enforced programmatically.

## Communicating exclusivity

"Founder access is limited to 25 accounts. We're building this with a small group of real service business owners before opening it up."

Do not manufacture urgency. The exclusivity is real.
