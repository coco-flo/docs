## Cohort targets

|---|---|
| Total seats | 25 |

## Application form

Built in Tally.so (OPS-8). Shared via DM only — not posted publicly. Toggle on/off natively in Tally.

## Evaluation criteria

1. Willing to run 50%+ of quote-to-invoice volume through Clarely during founder period
2. Business type and size fit cohort composition targets
3. Has existing clients and active quoting/invoicing workflow (not a startup)
4. Response quality — thoughtful answers signal genuine engagement

## Rejection language (standard)

"At this time you do not fit the current profile. We will retain your information and reach out if an opportunity opens."

## Acceptance workflow

1. Review submission against cohort tracker (Clarely_Founder_Cohort_Tracker.xlsx)
2. Confirm acceptance decision
3. Send early adopter agreement email (Founder Rate variant or Free Forever variant)
4. Await agreement confirmation
5. Create account manually or provide signup link
6. Set `is_early_adopter = true` and assign correct plan in Heimdall (CLA-6, CLA-8)
7. Update cohort tracker with status, start date, and plan type

## Volume commitment

Founders commit to running 50% of their quote-to-invoice volume through Clarely. This is a soft commitment tracked via monthly surveys — not enforced programmatically.
