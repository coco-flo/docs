## Application form

Built in Tally.so (OPS-8). Share via DM only — not posted publicly. Toggle on/off natively in Tally.

Application fields: name, business name, business type, team size, email, question: "How do you currently manage quotes and invoices?", question: "What percentage of your quote-to-invoice volume are you willing to run through Clarely during the program?", question: "What made you interested in Clarely?"

## Evaluation criteria

1. Willing to run 50%\+ of quote-to-invoice volume through Clarely during founder period
2. Business type and size fit cohort composition targets
3. Has existing clients and active quoting/invoicing workflow (not a startup without clients)
4. Response quality — thoughtful answers signal genuine engagement
5. Check current cohort tracker before accepting — don't exceed composition targets

## Rejection language

> "Thank you for your interest in Clarely's Founder Program. At this time you don't fit the current profile we're building — it's not a reflection on your business, just the specific mix we're looking for right now. We'll keep your information and reach out if an opportunity opens up."

## Acceptance workflow

1. Review submission against [cohort tracker guide](/internal/founder-program/cohort-tracker-guide) — confirm slot available in correct type/size/batch
2. Confirm acceptance decision
3. Determine cohort type: Founder Rate or Free Forever (see [Free Forever variant](/internal/founder-program/free-forever-variant))
4. Send appropriate [acceptance email](/internal/founder-program/acceptance-email-templates)
5. Await agreement confirmation (reply to email, or explicit "I agree" in group channel)
6. Create account — either provide signup link or create manually
7. In Heimdall: set plan to `business` (Founder Rate) or `free` (Free Forever), set `is_early_adopter = true` **Note:** CLA-6 (is\_early\_adopter flag) and CLA-8 (Heimdall early adopter UI) must be shipped before this step is operational. Check current status before onboarding first founder.
8. Set `trial_ends_at` to a far-future date (5\+ years) to exclude from trial expiry cron
9. Update cohort tracker: status = Active, start date, plan type, batch
10. Send [welcome message and onboarding checklist](/internal/founder-program/founder-onboarding-checklist)
11. Add to founder group channel
12. Set calendar reminder for Month 1 survey (30 days from start date)

## Volume commitment

Founders commit to running 50% of their quote-to-invoice volume through Clarely. Soft commitment — tracked via monthly surveys, not enforced programmatically. Don't chase compliance mechanically; focus on engagement quality.

## Communicating exclusivity

When reaching out cold: "Founder access is limited to 25 accounts. We're building this with a small group of real service business owners before opening it up."

Do not create a sense of urgency that feels artificial. The exclusivity is real.
