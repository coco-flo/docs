## What it is

Free Forever is a cohort variant for specific individuals — close contacts, advisors, or cases where paying for access doesn't make sense. These founders receive permanent access to the Clarely Free plan with no expectation of conversion to a paid plan.

They are full participants in the founder program: they join the group channel, complete monthly surveys, and provide feedback. They just don't convert to a paid plan at month 6.

## Who gets it

Free Forever is awarded by explicit decision. It's not the default for any cold outreach. Typical cases:

- Close personal contacts who are genuinely using the product but where charging would be awkward
- Advisors or collaborators contributing meaningfully to the product
- Edge cases where the person's business doesn't match the Founder Rate model but their feedback is uniquely valuable

Do not offer Free Forever to avoid a difficult conversation about pricing. If someone isn't a good fit for Founder Rate, they should receive standard pricing or a rejection.

## Plan setup

- Stripe: No subscription created. No credit card collected.
- App plan: `free`
- `is_early_adopter`: `true`
- `trial_ends_at`: Set to a far-future date (5\+ years) to exclude from trial expiry cron
- In Heimdall: displays as "Free" plan with early adopter flag visible

## What they can access

The Free plan with `is_early_adopter = true` means:

- Standard Free tier feature limits apply (100 quotes/month cap, 50 client cap, no email delivery, no digital signature)
- The early adopter flag is for internal tracking and support identification only — it does not expand feature access

**If you intend to give a Free Forever founder access to more features**, they need to be on the Founder Rate (\$20/month, Business tier) or a higher paid plan, not Free Forever. Free Forever is genuinely free-tier access, no more.

## Acceptance email

Use the Free Forever variant of the [acceptance email templates](/internal/founder-program/acceptance-email-templates).

## Survey participation

Free Forever founders participate in the same 6-month survey cadence. Survey compliance is tracked in the cohort tracker but has no billing consequence (there is no billing to revert). Track compliance for product feedback value, not enforcement.

## Conversion

At month 6, Free Forever founders are not given a conversion pitch. They are thanked and remain on Free access. If they express interest in upgrading at any point, direct them to the standard plan pricing page.

## Tracking in the cohort tracker

In the cohort tracker, Free Forever founders are recorded with:

- Plan column: `free-forever`
- Cohort type: Free Forever
- Notes: reason for Free Forever designation
