Complete every step in order. This process runs once per founder.

## Step 1: Account creation

- Provide signup link: `clarely.app/signup`
- OR create account manually if founder prefers
- Confirm account is created and email is verified

## Step 2: Heimdall configuration

- Open Heimdall and find the account by email
- Set plan: Founder Rate cohort = `business` / Free Forever cohort = `free`
- Set `is_early_adopter = true` _(requires CLA-6 to be shipped — check status before running)_
- Set `trial_ends_at` to a date 5\+ years in the future
- Confirm CLR account number assigned — note it in the cohort tracker

## Step 3: Cohort tracker update

- Open Clarely\_Founder\_Cohort\_Tracker.xlsx
- Add or update row: Status = Active, start date, plan type, batch, CLR account number
- Confirm cohort composition targets still met

## Step 4: Welcome message

Send via the founder group channel or email:

---

\[First name\], your account is live.

**App URL:** clarely.app — open in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android) and add to your home screen. This is how you'll get notifications when clients sign.

**Your account:** You're on \[Business/Free\] access. \[If Founder Rate: You have full access to every feature.\]

**Getting started:** The help docs are at docs.clarely.co. "Send your first quote" is the best place to start.

**Bugs and questions:** Reply here or email [support@clarely.co](mailto:support@clarely.co). If something isn't working, tell me directly — no ticket system, no bot.

**Monthly surveys:** I'll send a short survey each month starting \[Month 1 date\]. 5 minutes, direct questions. Your responses keep your Founder Rate.

Thanks for being part of this.

Cody

---

## Step 5: Group channel

- Add founder to the founder group channel
- Confirm they can see and post in the channel

## Step 6: Calendar reminders

- Month 1 survey due — \[Start date \+ 30 days\]
- Month 5 conversion outreach — \[Start date \+ 150 days\]
- Month 6 compliance audit — \[Start date \+ 180 days\]

## Step 7: Stripe (Founder Rate only)

- Confirm Founder Rate subscription is set up or scheduled to start at month 6
- Verify subscription uses the Founder Rate price ID (stored in Bitwarden)
- Confirm no charge fires during the 6-month free period

## Done

Founder is onboarded when all steps are checked.
