Complete every step in order before considering a founder fully onboarded. This process runs once per founder.

## Step 1: Account creation

- Provide signup link: `clarely.app/signup`, or create account manually if founder prefers
- Confirm account is created and email is verified

## Step 2: Heimdall configuration

- Open Heimdall and find the account by email
- Set plan: `business` (Founder Rate) or `free` (Free Forever)
- Set `is_early_adopter = true` _(requires CLA-6 to be shipped — check status before running)_
- Set `trial_ends_at` to a date 5\+ years in the future
- Confirm CLR account number is assigned — note it in the cohort tracker

## Step 3: Cohort tracker update

- Open Clarely\_Founder\_Cohort\_Tracker.xlsx
- Add or update row: Status = Active, start date = today, plan type, batch, CLR number
- Confirm cohort composition targets still met

## Step 4: Welcome message

Send via the founder group channel or email:

---

\[First name\], your account is live.

**App URL:** clarely.app — open in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android) and add to your home screen for push notifications when clients sign.

**Your account:** You're on \[Business/Free\] access. \[If Founder Rate: You have full access to every feature.\]

**Getting started:** Help docs at docs.clarely.co. "Send your first quote" is the best starting point.

**Bugs and questions:** Reply here or email [support@clarely.co](mailto:support@clarely.co). Direct to me — no ticket system, no bot.

**Monthly surveys:** First one coming \[Month 1 date\]. 5 minutes, direct questions. Your responses keep your Founder Rate.

Thanks for being part of this.

Cody

---

## Step 5: Group channel

- Add founder to the founder group channel (platform: \[INSERT PLATFORM once decided\])
- Confirm they can see and post

## Step 6: Calendar reminders

- Month 1 survey due — \[Start date \+ 30 days\]
- Month 5 conversion outreach — \[Start date \+ 150 days\]
- Month 6 compliance audit — \[Start date \+ 180 days\]

## Step 7: Stripe (Founder Rate only)

- Confirm Stripe Founder Rate subscription scheduled to start at month 6
- Verify Price ID matches Bitwarden record
- Confirm no charge fires during the 6-month free period

## Done

Founder is onboarded when all steps above are checked.
