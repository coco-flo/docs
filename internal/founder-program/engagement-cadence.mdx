## Overview

Each founder goes through a 6-month engagement program starting from their account creation date. Surveys run on each founder's individual timeline. Compliance threshold: 5 of 6 surveys (80%) to retain Founder Rate.

Survey tooling: Google Forms for monthly surveys. Tally.so for bug report and feature request forms.

## Survey schedule

| Month | Name | Trigger | Focus |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Onboarding | 30 days | First impressions, setup friction |
| 2 | Workflow | 60 days | Real usage patterns |
| 3 | Mid-point | 90 days | Satisfaction, feature adoption |
| 4 | Depth | 120 days | Advanced usage, gaps |
| 5 | Competitive | 150 days | Alternatives, NPS, churn risk |
| 6 | Conversion | 180 days | Overall experience, audit trigger |

## Survey questions by month

**Month 1 — Onboarding**

1. Did you send your first quote in Clarely? (Yes / Not yet)
2. If yes: what went well? If not: what got in the way?
3. Was anything confusing or unclear when you first set up your account?
4. On a scale of 1-5, how easy was the initial setup?
5. Anything else you want to share from your first month?

**Month 2 — Workflow**

1. How many quotes have you sent through Clarely so far? (0 / 1-5 / 6-20 / 20\+)
2. Are you using Clarely as your primary tool for quotes and invoices?
3. What features are you using most?
4. Is there anything you expected to be able to do that you can't?
5. Any bugs or issues to report?

**Month 3 — Mid-point**

1. On a scale of 1-10, how satisfied are you with Clarely so far?
2. Which features have made the most difference to how you work?
3. If Clarely shut down tomorrow, how would you feel? (No problem / Somewhat inconvenient / Major disruption)
4. What's the one thing that would make Clarely significantly more useful?
5. Any other feedback?

**Month 4 — Depth**

1. Are you using recurring jobs? (Yes / No / N/A)
2. Are you using team members in Clarely? (Yes / No / N/A)
3. Is there any part of your workflow you still do outside Clarely? What and why?
4. Have you had any client disputes about scope? Did a signed quote help?
5. What would make you more likely to recommend Clarely to another business owner?

**Month 5 — Competitive**

1. Before Clarely, what tool or method did you use for quotes and invoices?
2. Have you looked at any other tools while using Clarely?
3. What is Clarely's biggest advantage over alternatives?
4. What is Clarely's biggest gap compared to alternatives?
5. Would you recommend Clarely to another service business owner? (Definitely / Probably / Not sure / No)

**Month 6 — Conversion**

1. Overall, how has Clarely fit into your business over these 6 months?
2. What's the most valuable thing Clarely has done for your business?
3. What's the biggest remaining gap in the product for your needs?
4. Are you planning to continue using Clarely going forward? (Yes / No / Not sure)
5. Is there anything you'd want me to know before we close out the formal program period?

## Month 5 conversion outreach

Begin personal outreach in parallel with the month 5 survey. Don't wait for the response.

> "\[Name\], we're getting close to the end of the 6-month program. I wanted to check in personally. How has it been going overall? Are you planning to continue with Clarely after month 6?"

If yes: "Great. Your Founder Rate stays at \$20/month. I'll set up the Stripe subscription to start on \[date\]. You won't need to do anything."

If no or not sure: "I appreciate the honesty. What's driving that? Is it the product, the pricing, or something else?"

Log the outcome in the cohort tracker. If yes, verify Stripe is configured.

## Month 6 compliance audit

After the month 6 survey window closes (7-day grace):

1. Count responses per founder
2. 5\+ responses: retain Founder Rate
3. 4 or fewer: personal outreach, 7-day additional grace, then revert if unresolved

Reversion message:

> "\[Name\], after the 6-month program, your plan is being updated to standard pricing at \$79/month starting \[date\]. This is because the program required responses to 5 of 6 surveys, and we didn't receive enough to maintain the Founder Rate. I reached out in \[month\] about this. Reply if you have questions."

## Handling silent founders

Miss 1 survey: no action. Miss 2 consecutive: reach out in group channel. Miss 3\+: email directly, mark Non-responsive in tracker. Continue sending surveys. Apply normal month 6 audit. Don't cancel until month 6 or explicit request.
