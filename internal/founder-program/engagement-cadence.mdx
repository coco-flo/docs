## Overview

Each founder goes through a 6-month engagement program starting from their account creation date. Surveys are sent on each founder's individual timeline, not a single calendar date.

Compliance threshold: 5 of 6 surveys (80%) to retain Founder Rate.

Survey tooling: Google Forms for monthly surveys (no branching needed, simpler to iterate). Tally.so for the bug report and feature request forms.

## Survey schedule

| Month | Survey name | Trigger | Focus |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Onboarding | 30 days after start | First impressions, setup friction, confusion points |
| 2 | Workflow | 60 days | Real usage patterns, what they're actually doing |
| 3 | Mid-point | 90 days | Satisfaction, feature adoption, conversion readiness |
| 4 | Depth | 120 days | Advanced feature usage, what they still do outside Clarely |
| 5 | Competitive | 150 days | Alternatives they considered, NPS, churn risk signals |
| 6 | Conversion | 180 days | Overall experience, recommendation likelihood, audit trigger |

## Survey questions by month

**Month 1 - Onboarding**

1. Did you send your first quote in Clarely? (Yes / Not yet)
2. If yes: what went well? If not: what got in the way?
3. Was anything confusing or unclear when you first set up your account?
4. On a scale of 1-5, how easy was the initial setup? (1 = very difficult, 5 = very easy)
5. Anything else you want to share from your first month?

**Month 2 - Workflow**

1. How many quotes have you sent through Clarely so far? (0 / 1-5 / 6-20 / 20\+)
2. What's your current workflow for quotes and invoices? Are you using Clarely as your primary tool?
3. What features are you using most?
4. Is there anything you expected to be able to do that you can't?
5. Any bugs or issues to report?

**Month 3 - Mid-point**

1. On a scale of 1-10, how satisfied are you with Clarely so far?
2. Which features have made the most difference to how you work?
3. If Clarely shut down tomorrow, how would you feel? (No problem / Somewhat inconvenient / Major disruption)
4. What's the one thing that would make Clarely significantly more useful for your business?
5. Any feedback on the product or experience to share?

**Month 4 - Depth**

1. Are you using recurring jobs? (Yes / No / Not applicable)
2. Are you using team members in Clarely? (Yes / No / Not applicable)
3. Is there any part of your quote-to-invoice workflow that you still do outside Clarely? What and why?
4. Have you had any client disputes or difficult conversations about scope? Did having a signed quote help?
5. What would make you more likely to recommend Clarely to another business owner?

**Month 5 - Competitive**

1. Before Clarely, what tool or method did you use for quotes and invoices?
2. Have you looked at any other tools while using Clarely? Which ones?
3. What is Clarely's biggest advantage over those alternatives?
4. What is Clarely's biggest gap compared to alternatives?
5. Would you recommend Clarely to another service business owner? (Definitely / Probably / Not sure / No)

**Month 6 - Conversion**

1. Overall, how has Clarely fit into your business over these 6 months?
2. What's the most valuable thing Clarely has done for your business?
3. What's the biggest remaining gap in the product for your needs?
4. Are you planning to continue using Clarely going forward? (Yes / No / Not sure)
5. Is there anything you'd want me to know before we close out the formal program period?

## Month 5 conversion outreach protocol

At the Month 5 survey, begin personal conversion outreach in parallel. Don't wait for the survey response.

Initial message:

> "\[Name\], we're getting close to the end of the 6-month program. I wanted to check in personally. How has it been going overall? Are you planning to continue with Clarely after month 6?"

If the answer is yes:

> "Great. Your Founder Rate stays at \$20/month. I'll set up the Stripe subscription to start on \[date\]. You won't need to do anything."

If the answer is no or not sure:

> "I appreciate the honesty. What's driving that? Is it the product, the pricing, or something else? I'm asking because I genuinely want to understand, not to push back."

Log the outcome in the cohort tracker. If yes, confirm Stripe is configured correctly.

## Month 6 compliance audit

After the Month 6 survey window closes (7-day grace period):

1. Count responses per founder in the cohort tracker
2. 5\+ responses: Retain Founder Rate. No action.
3. 4 or fewer responses: Personal outreach. 7-day additional grace period. Then revert to standard pricing if unresolved.

Reversion notification:

> "\[Name\], I wanted to let you know that after the 6-month program, your plan is being updated to standard pricing at \$79/month starting \[date\]. This is because the program required responses to 5 of 6 monthly surveys, and we didn't receive enough to maintain the Founder Rate. I reached out in \[month\] about this. If you have questions, reply to this email."

## Handling a founder who goes silent

Miss 1 survey: no action. Continue the cadence.

Miss 2 consecutive surveys: reach out via the group channel.

> "\[Name\], just checking in. Haven't heard from you in a bit and want to make sure everything is okay with Clarely. Are you still using it? Anything I should know?"

Miss 3\+ surveys: email directly. Mark as Non-responsive in tracker. Continue sending surveys. Apply normal month 6 audit.

Do not remove a silent founder from the program until month 6. A founder who was using the product for months 1-3 and went quiet is different from one who never engaged.
