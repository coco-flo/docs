## Overview

Each founder goes through a 6-month engagement program starting from their account creation date. Surveys are sent manually on each founder's individual timeline — not a single calendar date.

## Compliance threshold

Founders must respond to **5 of 6 surveys** (80%) to retain Founder Rate at the end of the 6-month period. Track compliance in Clarely_Founder_Cohort_Tracker.xlsx.

## Survey schedule

| Month | Survey name | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Onboarding | ~30 days after account creation |
| 2 | Workflow | ~60 days |
| 3 | Mid-point check-in | ~90 days |
| 4 | Depth | ~120 days |
| 5 | Competitive | ~150 days — also trigger conversion outreach |
| 6 | Conversion | ~180 days — final compliance audit |

## Month 5 conversion outreach

At the Month 5 survey, begin personal conversion outreach in parallel. The goal is to have a committed yes/no before the 6-month period closes. Do not wait for Month 6 survey to start the conversation.

## Month 6 compliance audit

After Month 6 survey window closes (allow 7-day grace period):

1. Count survey responses per founder in cohort tracker
2. Founders at 5+ responses: retain Founder Rate, no action
3. Founders at 4 or fewer responses: personal outreach explaining the compliance threshold, then revert to standard pricing if no resolution within 7 days

## Survey tooling

Built in Tally.so (OPS-9). One form per month. Toggle on/off independently per survey. Sent via email or group channel message with direct link.
