This walks you through sending your first quote end to end — from creating it to getting your client's signature.

## Before you start

Make sure you've added your business name in [Account Settings](/account/business-settings). It appears on every quote and invoice you send.

## Create the quote

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Quotes">
    From the main menu, tap **Quotes**, then tap **New Quote**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your client">
    Enter your client's name and email address. The email is where Clarely sends the quote for signature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add line items">
    Add each service or item with a description, quantity, and price. Be specific — this is the scope your client is signing. What's in the quote is what's covered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and send">
    Review the total, then tap **Send Quote**. Your client receives an email with a link to review and sign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What your client sees

Your client gets an email with a link to their quote. They review every line item, see the total, and sign digitally. Once they sign, the scope is locked — you both have a record of exactly what was agreed.

## After the client signs

You get a notification when the client signs. The quote moves to **Accepted** status. From there you can:

- **Convert to a job** — schedule the work and track it to completion
- **Invoice immediately** — for professional services work where no job step is needed

<Tip>
  Install Clarely on your home screen so you see signature notifications the moment they come in. See [Install on your phone](/getting-started/pwa-install).
</Tip>
