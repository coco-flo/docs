Once your quote is ready, sending it takes one tap. Clarely emails your client a link to review and sign.

## How to send

From a draft quote, tap **Send Quote**. Clarely sends your client an email with a secure link to their quote.

## What the client receives

The client gets an email from Clarely with:

- Your business name
- A summary of the quote total
- A link to review the full quote and sign

The link takes them to a mobile-friendly page where they can read every line item and sign digitally. They don't need a Clarely account.

## Resending a quote

If your client hasn't signed and needs the quote resent, open the quote and tap **Resend**. A new email goes to the same address with the same link.

## Changing a sent quote

Once a quote is sent, you can't edit line items or pricing — doing so would change what the client is about to sign. If the scope changes before signature, you have two options:

- **Cancel and recreate** — cancel the sent quote and create a new one with the updated scope
- **Wait for decline** — if the client declines, you can revise and resend

After a client signs, the quote is locked and cannot be changed.

<Note>
  Quote emails come from Clarely's sending domain. Make sure your client checks their spam folder if they don't see the email within a few minutes.
</Note>
