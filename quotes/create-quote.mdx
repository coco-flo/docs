<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Quotes">
    From the main menu, tap **Quotes**, then tap **New Quote**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a client">
    Choose a client from your client list. If the client doesn't exist yet, add them first from the **Clients** section — you'll need their name and email address at minimum.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add line items">
    Add each service or item. See [Line items](/quotes/line-items) for detail on how to structure them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set tax, terms, and deposit">
    Optionally apply a tax rate, choose your contract terms (standard or enhanced), and configure a deposit if you want one collected before the job.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an acceptance flow">
    Select whether this quote should follow the **field service flow** (Quote → Job → Invoice) or the **professional services flow** (Quote → Invoice immediately on acceptance). You can change this before sending.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or send">
    Tap **Save Draft** to save without sending, or go to the draft quote and tap **Send Quote** when ready. The client receives an email with a link to review and sign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quote statuses

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Draft | Created but not yet sent |
| Sent | Sent to client, awaiting signature |
| Accepted | Client has signed — scope is locked |
| Declined | Client declined the quote |

## Editing a quote

Quotes can only be edited while in **Draft** status. Once sent, the quote is locked for the client's review. If you need to make changes after sending, the cleanest option is to cancel the sent quote and create a new one with the updated details.

Once a client signs (Accepted), the quote is permanently frozen — no edits are possible by anyone. See [Scope protection](/quotes/scope-protection).

## Notes

Two note fields are available on a quote:

- **Owner notes** — visible only to you. Use these for job context, internal reminders, or anything your client shouldn't see.
- **Staff notes** — visible to team members assigned to the job. Use these for access instructions, site details, or anything the crew needs to know.

Clients never see either note field.

## Quote follow-up

If a sent quote hasn't been signed, Clarely can automatically follow up with your client after a set number of days. Configure follow-up timing and messaging in **Account** → **Quoting Defaults**.
