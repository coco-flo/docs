---
title: "create-quote"
description: "Build a new quote with line items and client details."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Quotes">
    From the main menu, tap **Quotes**, then tap **New Quote**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add client details">
    Enter the client's name and email. The email is required — it's where the quote is sent for signature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the quote type">
    Choose whether this quote will follow the field service flow (Quote → Job → Invoice) or the professional services flow (Quote → Invoice on acceptance). You can change this before sending.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add line items">
    Add each service or item. See [Line items](/quotes/line-items) for detail on how to structure them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and send">
    Check the total and review the line items, then tap **Send Quote**. The client receives an email immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quote statuses

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Draft | Created but not yet sent |
| Sent | Sent to client, awaiting signature |
| Accepted | Client has signed — scope is locked |
| Declined | Client declined the quote |
| Expired | Quote passed its expiry date unsigned |

## Notes and internal details

You can add internal notes to a quote that only you and your team see — clients never see these. Use them for job context, access instructions, or reminders.
