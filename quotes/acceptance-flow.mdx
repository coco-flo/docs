When a client signs a quote, Clarely moves it to **Accepted** status and notifies you immediately. What happens next depends on the workflow type you selected when creating the quote.

## Field service flow

For field service businesses (cleaning, trades, maintenance), acceptance triggers the job creation step:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Quote accepted">
    Client signs. You receive a notification. The quote status changes to Accepted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Convert to job">
    Open the accepted quote and tap **Create Job**. The job inherits the quote details automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule and complete">
    Schedule the job, assign team members if needed, and mark it complete when the work is done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate invoice">
    From the completed job, tap **Generate Invoice**. The invoice pulls line items and pricing from the original signed quote.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Professional services flow

For professional services work where the deliverable doesn't require a scheduled job:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Quote accepted">
    Client signs. You receive a notification. The quote status changes to Accepted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invoice immediately">
    Clarely can generate an invoice directly from the accepted quote. Tap **Generate Invoice** from the quote.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notifications

You receive a push notification when a client signs. Make sure Clarely is installed on your home screen and notifications are enabled. See [Install on your phone](/getting-started/pwa-install).
