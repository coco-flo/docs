Team members are the people who work jobs in your business. They can view their assigned jobs, update job status, and access the schedule from their own devices.

## Adding a team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account">
    Open **Account** → **Team Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add Team Member">
    Enter the team member's name and email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invite">
    Tap **Send Invite**. The team member receives an email to set up their Clarely account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What team members can do

Team members have a limited view of Clarely focused on their work:

- See their assigned jobs and schedule
- Update job status (In Progress, Completed)
- View job details and client location

Team members cannot:

- Create or send quotes
- Generate or send invoices
- Access billing or account settings
- See unassigned jobs or other team members' schedules

## Removing a team member

Go to **Account** → **Team Members**, find the team member, and tap **Remove**. Their access is revoked immediately. Jobs they were assigned to remain assigned — reassign them if needed.

## Team member limits

The number of team members you can add depends on your plan. See [Plan and billing](/account/plan-billing) for details.
