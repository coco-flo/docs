Team members are the people who work jobs in your business. They get access to their assigned jobs, can update job status and add field notes, and log in through a separate staff portal — no access to your quotes, invoices, or account settings.

## Adding a team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team settings">
    Open **Account** → **Settings** → **Team**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite the team member">
    Enter their first name, last name, and email address, then tap **Send Invite**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They set up access">
    Your team member receives a welcome email. When they're ready to log in, they go to your staff portal (see below) and sign in with their email using a one-time code.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The staff portal

Team members don't log in through clarely.app. They use a separate **staff portal** — a dedicated URL tied to your business.

Your staff portal URL is based on your business slug, which you can find and customize in **Account** → **Settings** → **Business**.

**How team members log in:**

1. They open your staff portal URL in a browser (or save it to their home screen)
2. They enter their email address
3. Clarely emails them a one-time access code
4. They enter the code to sign in

There's no password to set up or remember. Each login generates a fresh code sent to their email. Sessions last up to 10 hours.

## What team members can do

Team members see only what they need to do their job:

- View their assigned jobs and job details
- See client name, address, and phone number for assigned jobs
- Update job status (In Progress, Completed)
- Add and edit field notes on in-progress jobs
- Update their own name

Team members cannot:

- See unassigned jobs or other team members' jobs
- View quotes, invoices, or pricing
- Access billing or account settings
- See owner notes (only staff notes are visible)

## Disabling a team member

Go to **Account** → **Settings** → **Team**, find the team member, and tap **Disable**. Their access is revoked immediately — their current session stops working on the next action. Jobs they were assigned to remain assigned; reassign them if needed.

## Staff notes vs owner notes

When creating a quote or job, you have two note fields:

- **Owner notes** — private to you only, never visible to team members
- **Staff notes** — visible to the assigned team member through the staff portal

Use staff notes for anything the crew needs on-site: gate codes, parking instructions, what to avoid, client preferences.

## Team member limits

The number of team members you can add depends on your plan. See [Plan and billing](/account/plan-billing) for details.
