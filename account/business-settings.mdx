Your business settings control what clients see on quotes and invoices. Set these up before sending your first quote.

## Accessing business settings

Go to **Account** → **Business Settings**.

## What to configure

**Business name** — appears on every quote, invoice, and email sent from Clarely. Use the name your clients recognize.

**Contact email** — the reply-to email on client-facing emails from Clarely. Make sure this is a monitored inbox.

**Phone number** — displayed on quotes and invoices as a contact option for clients.

**Business address** — your primary business address. Appears on invoices for billing reference.

**Logo** — upload your business logo to display on quotes and invoices. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, SVG. Recommended size: at least 300px wide.

## Saving changes

Changes to business settings take effect immediately. Any new quotes or invoices generated after saving will reflect the updated details. Existing sent quotes and invoices are not affected.

<Note>
  Your business name is the most important field to get right before your first quote. Clients see it immediately and it appears on every document.
</Note>
