Clarely offers several plans depending on the size of your business and the features you need.

## Available plans

| Plan | Price | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | \$0/mo | Getting started, low volume |
| Starter | \$15/mo | Solo operators with regular clients |
| Professional | \$29/mo | Growing solo businesses |
| Team | \$49/mo | Small teams up to a few members |
| Business | \$79/mo | Larger teams, full feature access |

Annual billing saves 2 months compared to monthly billing.

## Viewing your current plan

Go to **Account** → **Plan & Billing** to see your current plan, billing cycle, and next payment date.

## Changing your plan

Tap **Change Plan** to upgrade or downgrade. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

## Updating payment details

Tap **Update Payment Method** to change the card on file. You'll be directed to a secure Stripe-hosted form to update your billing information.

## Cancelling your subscription

Tap **Cancel Plan** to cancel your subscription. Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your account moves to the Free plan — your data is retained.

<Note>
  If you're on the Founder Rate, your plan details reflect the special pricing and terms from your founder agreement. Contact support if you have questions about your founder plan.
</Note>
