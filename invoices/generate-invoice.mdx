---
title: "generate-invoice"
description: "Create an invoice from a completed job or accepted quote."
---

Clarely generates invoices from the work you've already recorded — no manual entry required.

## From a completed job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the completed job">
    Go to **Jobs**, find the completed job, and open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Generate Invoice">
    Tap **Generate Invoice**. Clarely creates the invoice with line items and pricing pulled directly from the original signed quote.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    Confirm the line items and total. Add any notes for the client if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send or save">
    Tap **Send Invoice** to send immediately, or **Save as Draft** to send later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## From an accepted quote (professional services)

For professional services work where no job is needed:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the accepted quote">
    Go to **Quotes**, find the accepted quote.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Generate Invoice">
    Tap **Generate Invoice** directly from the accepted quote.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and send">
    Confirm and send.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What carries over automatically

- All line items from the signed quote
- Descriptions, quantities, and pricing
- Client name and email
- Tax (if configured in account settings)

You cannot change line items or pricing on a generated invoice — they're locked to the signed quote. If you need to adjust, create a new quote for the additional or changed work.
