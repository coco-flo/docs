Every invoice in Clarely has a status that tells you exactly where it stands.

## Status definitions

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Draft | Created but not yet sent to the client |
| Sent | Sent to client, awaiting payment |
| Paid | Payment received and confirmed |
| Overdue | Past due date, payment not received |
| Written Off | Marked uncollectible — removed from active tracking |

## How statuses update

Most status changes happen automatically:

- **Draft → Sent**: when you tap Send Invoice
- **Sent → Paid**: when Stripe confirms payment, or when you tap Mark as Paid manually
- **Sent → Overdue**: automatically the day after the due date passes

Manual status changes:

- **Sent/Overdue → Paid**: tap Mark as Paid and enter payment details
- **Any → Written Off**: tap Write Off on the invoice

## Filtering by status

In your invoice list, use the filter controls to view invoices by status. Filtering by **Overdue** shows everything that needs follow-up. Filtering by **Paid** shows your collected revenue.
