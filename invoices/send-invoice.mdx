Once an invoice is generated, sending it takes one tap. Your client receives an email with a link to pay.

## How to send

From any draft invoice, tap **Send Invoice**. Clarely sends your client an email with a secure payment link.

## What the client receives

Your client gets an email with your business name, the invoice total, and a link to pay online. The payment page shows the full invoice with every line item.

## Resending an invoice

If your client needs the invoice resent, open the invoice and tap **Resend**. A new email goes to the same address.

## Sending to a different email

If you need to send the invoice to a different email than the one on the quote, open the invoice, tap **Edit Client Email**, update the address, and send.

## Manual payment recording

If your client pays by cash, check, or bank transfer outside of Clarely, you can mark the invoice as paid manually. Open the invoice and tap **Mark as Paid**. Enter the payment method and date.

<Note>
  Manual payment marking does not process any funds — it only updates the invoice status in Clarely for your records.
</Note>
